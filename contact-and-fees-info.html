Paying for Psychotherapy
As a Fee For Service (FFS) provider, I do not contract with any health insurers.
If you have out-of-network coverage, you may be able to get a portion of your fees reimbursed.
Patients use cash/check/debit/health spending/credit card to pay at the end of a session.
90-minute intake interview, and 60-minute psychotherapy session cost $200/encounter.
A 25% fee reduction is offered to those who need help, but can not afford full rate.
A 50% rate is offered to Psy.D, Ph.D, Ed.D students, who commit to fulfill their therapy requirement with me.
​Talk to me about your financial circumstances, and I will try to help you out!
